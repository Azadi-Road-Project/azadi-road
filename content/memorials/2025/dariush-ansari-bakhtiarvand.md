One of his relatives has announced that Ansari Bakhtiarvand was shot during the protests and died before reaching the hospital. The head of the Isfahan provincial police, without giving further details, investigated the death of the "27-year-old citizen" in Foladshahr on the evening of January 1. The human rights organization Hengaw has also reported that he was killed by direct fire from security forces.
